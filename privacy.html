Zero data access. We mean it.

2. Hosting & Infrastructure (Cloudflare)

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. To ensure a secure and stable connection, Cloudflare processes technical data (IP addresses, device information) in log files. This is based on our legitimate interest (Art. 6 Para. 1 lit. f GDPR).

3. Local Fonts

To protect your privacy, we self-host all web fonts locally on our own server. No connection is made to Google servers, meaning your IP address is not transmitted to Google for font rendering.

4. Your Rights

According to the GDPR, you have the right to access (Art. 15), rectification (Art. 16), erasure (Art. 17), restriction of processing (Art. 18), and data portability (Art. 20). You can object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interests (Art. 21).

5. Gmail Script Security

The DontMailMe worker script executes locally within your own Google account. We never receive, process, or store your emails, tokens, or any other personal data from your Google account.
